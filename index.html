Dog Collector

  • High Concept: A dog owner trying to get the unleashed dogs back.
  • Player Experience Goals: Full concentration to keep track of the dogs' movement.
  • Genre: Casual arcade game.
  • Game play: The player can move around using the arrow keys and catch the dogs if he/she collides with them. The goal of this game is to catch all 10 dogs in the park.
  • Prototype goal: Catch all the dogs.
  • Features: Dogs move very fast and they are hard to catch.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting of this game is a park. A person who owns the dogs and 10 dogs are the characters in this game.
  • Story: The owner of the dogs suddenly realized that he forgot to turn off the oven at home while he was enjoying a picnic at the park. However, the dogs are unleashed and the owner has to leash all of them to get back home.
  • Target Audience: All age groups are targeted for this game.
  • Play time: It only takes couple minutes.
  • Strategies: It would be a good idea to pay attention to the dogs' moving angles and read their paths.
